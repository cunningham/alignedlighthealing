I get it

Figuring out how to care for what your body needs can be overwhelming, and it’s really tough to know where to start. Especially if you’re dealing with chronic pain!

As an adult Adhd human living with hyper-mobility, POTS, and a variety of other chronic conditions, I truly understand what it’s like to try to keep up with everything life throws at you while feeling constantly exhausted.

I know that it can feel impossible to find a moment of calm and stillness in the whirlwind of things that are constantly trying to get your attention. That most days feel like you have to force yourself to keep going and you never know how far you you’re going to get before you crash.

Here’s the thing…

I’ve learned over the last few years of my healing journey that even though it is hard, it IS possible to make growth and healing a priority in the midst of the chaos. When you are intentional about setting aside the time to make sure that your own needs are met, other things in your life are able to shift. You begin to have new opportunities to give yourself the love, compassion, and healing that you deserve simply because you exist… And that changes everything.

You matter.

Your needs matter.

And you deserve to live a supported and fulfilling life.
