Scheduling an Appointment

Available by appointment only

1 hour appointment - $100

Each appointment includes 50 minutes of focused work on the massage table and 5-10 minutes at both the beginning and at the end of your time to discuss goals and your personalized treatment plan.

Please plan for a maximum of 75 minutes for your appointment time.

Important Notes

You will stay fully clothed for the whole appointment so please come dressed in loose comfortable clothing. Preferably no jeans.

Since this is very different than a typical Swedish massage, please reach our if you have any questions. I’d love to talk with you about what you’re hoping to get out of your appointment!
